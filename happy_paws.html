Vacation for them — Peace of mind for you

Hello, we’re Happy Paws, your new dog sitting service. We know you’re busy. Work, travel, vacation, no one wants to leave their pet alone. So let us take care of that, we’re really good at it, we promise!

Dog Walking

Need someone to check in on your pet and make sure they are getting the exercies they need? We have you covered! We will come to your home and take your pet for a 30 minute walk.

Daycare

Go about your day with peace of mind knowing your pet is in good hands with lots of playtime and regular bathroom breaks. Oh, and maybe a few extra treats as well!

Overnight

We would be happy to have your pet for a sleepover! There is always staff onsite overnight to make sure your pet is comfortable and happy.

Grooming

We offer a variety of grooming services from nail clippings, haircuts, bathing, and de-shedding. They will leave looking better than when they came in!
